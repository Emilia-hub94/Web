What is an Migraines?

A migraine is much more than a bad headache. It can cause debilitating, throbbing, one-sided head pain that can leave you in bed for days. Movement, lights, sounds and other triggers may cause symptoms like fatigue, nausea, vision changes, irritability and more. read more..

Different Types Of Migraines.

There are 14 different types of migraines that are different from each other and have different affect on people. These are the different types of Migraines:

  • Migraine without aura
  • Migraine with aura
  • Chronic migraine
  • Abdominal migraine
  • Acephalgic migraine
  • Migraine with brainstem aura
  • Hemiplegic migraine
  • Retinal migraine
  • Status migrainosus
  • Menstrual migraine
  • Ophthalmoplegic migraine
  • Medication-induced migraine
  • Vestibular migraine
  • Cyclic vomiting syndrome
