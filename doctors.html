Doctors

Emer McGrath is an Associate Professor in the School of Medicine at University of Galway and Consultant Neurologist at University Hospital Galway. Professor McGrath received her medical degree from University of Galway in 2009 and completed her early medical training at University Hospital Galway. She completed a PhD in clinical epidemiology and biostatistics at University of Galway with a focus on vascular brain disorders and was co-PI and lead for the SLEPT clinical trial.
